About

Codedestination is an online platform for free education meant for Computer Science undergraduates. The website's main agenda is the growth of young soft techies by spreading out the seeds of knowledge through every pore possible of which this site is a miniature model.

We are building an online stage that aims :
  • * to impart cost-free knowledge
  • * educate and nurture the young minds
  • * to inculcate coding habits
